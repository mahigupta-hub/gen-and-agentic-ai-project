Chapter Summary

Photosynthesis is the biological process through which green plants prepare their own food. It uses sunlight, chlorophyll, water and carbon dioxide. Oxygen is released as a by-product. This process is essential because it provides food and oxygen for almost all living organisms.

📌 Key Points

  • Photosynthesis occurs inside chloroplasts.
  • Chlorophyll absorbs sunlight.
  • Carbon dioxide enters through stomata.
  • Water is absorbed from roots.
  • Oxygen is released into the atmosphere.

⭐ Important Terms

Chlorophyll Glucose Sunlight Carbon Dioxide Oxygen
